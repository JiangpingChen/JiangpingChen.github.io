Biography

Dr. Jiangping Chen is a Professor in the School of Information Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. She conducts interdisciplinary research, spanning information science, library science, data science, and health informatics.    (Curriculum Vitae)

Research Areas and Expertise

  • Data Analytics for Information Problems: Misinformation, Topic Modeling and Analysis, Knowledge Discovery, Machine Learning, Application of Large Language Models
  • Digital Libraries: Multilingual Digital Libraries and Collections, Technologies, Services, and Sustainability
  • Information Services: Library Services, Information User Studies
  • Information Retrieval: Cross Language Information Retrieval, Multilingual Information Retrieval and Access, Medical Information Retrieval, Applied Natural Language Processing

My Current Research Projects

  • Misinformation on Social Media
  • Artificial Intelligence for Digital Libraries
